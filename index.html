Vice President of SGA

I have been involved in student government since middle school. During the middle school student government, I held positions of leadership such as Senator during 6th grade, Vice President during 7th grade, and President during 8th grade. When I started high school and joined the high school student government, I was class representative up until the end of 11th grade. This year, I was elected Vice President of the Student Government Association. I enjoy participating in this extracurricular because it allows me to exercise my leadership skills and also gives me a chance to be involved in the school's life including school events, student concerns, faculty conversations, and the overall culture of the high school.

Concertmaster(1st Chair Violinist)

I am first chair in the first section violins, which means I am Concertmaster of the orchestra. I have grown to love music throughout the years that I've been able to learn how to play the violin. It provides me an alternate way to communicate my emotions. I also enjoy the leadership that I get to assume in my school's orchestra. Being able to help the entire orchestra do well, but also find value in music just as much as I do is exciting for me.
